二、5–10分钟小测

1. A writer who composes poems or songs may be called a ____.

  1. bard
  2. behemoth
  3. boon
  4. bias
显示答案

A. bard

2. If someone speaks in a loud, rowdy, energetic way, that person is ____.

  1. benign
  2. boisterous
  3. brusque
  4. bashful
显示答案

B. boisterous

3. A serious disaster or great misfortune is a ____.

  1. cadence
  2. battery
  3. calamity
  4. blemish
显示答案

C. calamity

4. If a statement sounds exaggerated and pompous, it is ____.

  1. bombastic
  2. benevolent
  3. bereft
  4. benign
显示答案

A. bombastic

5. If you beg someone sincerely for help, you ____ them.

  1. burnish
  2. beseech
  3. bilk
  4. buffet
显示答案

B. beseech

6. A small flaw on a surface is a ____.

  1. blight
  2. boon
  3. blemish
  4. bias
显示答案

C. blemish

7. If a judge has unfair prejudice, that is called ____.

  1. bias
  2. cadence
  3. cajole
  4. battery
显示答案

A. bias

8. To polish silver until it shines is to ____ it.

  1. berate
  2. burnish
  3. buttress
  4. bequeath
显示答案

B. burnish

四、阅读找词任务

When the small town prepared for its annual arts festival, the mayor asked a local bard to open the event with a short poem. At first, the young organizer felt bashful about addressing the crowd, but the poet’s benevolent smile helped calm her. Soon the quiet square turned boisterous as musicians, food vendors, and children filled the streets. One critic, however, gave a bombastic speech complaining about every tiny blemish in the plan. The mayor refused to berate the volunteers; instead, he tried to buttress their confidence by praising their hard work. By sunset, what some had feared would become a calamity had instead become a boon for the whole town.

1. Find a word meaning “kind and helpful.”

显示答案

benevolent

2. Find a word meaning “noisy and full of energy.”

显示答案

boisterous

3. Find a word meaning “a small flaw or imperfection.”

显示答案

blemish

4. Find a word meaning “to strengthen or support.”

显示答案

buttress

5. Find a word meaning “a disaster.”

显示答案

calamity

6. Find a word meaning “a benefit or blessing.”

显示答案

boon

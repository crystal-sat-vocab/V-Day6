二、5–10分钟小测

1. To support or strengthen an argument is to ____ it.

  1. bolster
  2. berate
  3. beseech
  4. blight
显示答案

A. bolster

2. A harsh, unpleasant mixture of sounds is a ____.

  1. brevity
  2. cacophony
  3. candor
  4. cache
显示答案

B. cacophony

3. A person who is honest and straightforward is ____.

  1. callous
  2. candid
  3. banal
  4. boorish
显示答案

B. candid

4. To surrender or give in is to ____.

  1. capitulate
  2. cajole
  3. burnish
  4. besiege
显示答案

A. capitulate

5. A speech that is pompous and inflated may be called ____.

  1. benign
  2. brazen
  3. bombastic
  4. bereft
显示答案

C. bombastic

6. A hidden store of goods is a ____.

  1. bane
  2. bias
  3. cache
  4. calumny
显示答案

C. cache

7. Someone rude and insensitive may be described as ____.

  1. boorish
  2. canonical
  3. benign
  4. candid
显示答案

A. boorish

8. To polish or improve a reputation is to ____.

  1. beseech
  2. burnish
  3. buttress
  4. blight
显示答案

B. burnish

Day 6 词汇卡｜146–174

146. bard

IPA 音标：/bɑːrd/

拼读：bard

中文：诗人；吟游诗人

英文：a poet, especially one who writes or recites verses

例句发音：

SAT例句：
Shakespeare is often regarded as the greatest bard in English literature.

中文翻译：
莎士比亚常被认为是英国文学中最伟大的诗人。

💡 SAT重点词汇老师讲解

SAT中常见于文学语境。看到 the Bard，常常特指 Shakespeare。

147. bashful

IPA 音标：/ˈbæʃ.fəl/

拼读：BASH-ful

中文：害羞的；腼腆的

英文：shy or easily embarrassed

例句发音：

SAT例句：
The bashful student spoke softly during the class presentation.

中文翻译：
这位害羞的学生在课堂展示时说话很轻。

💡 SAT重点词汇老师讲解

常与 shy, timid, reserved 相关。注意它通常不是负面词，而是性格害羞。

💡 SAT重点词汇老师讲解

SAT中可能考一词多义：battery 不只表示电池，也可表示“一连串”或“殴打”。

Day 6 SAT Reading｜The Bard's Final Gift

In a noisy town ruled by a bombastic mayor, a young bard named Milo tried to bring peace through music. The town had suffered a calamity when a storm damaged the old bridge, and the people were bereft of hope. A benevolent artisan offered help, but a brazen merchant tried to bilk the villagers by selling weak wood at a high price. Milo, though bashful, noticed the merchant’s bias against the poor. He decided to beseech the town council for help. At first, the council was brusque, but Milo did not balk. He used a gentle cadence in his speech and tried to cajole them with a ballad about courage and kindness. The song became a boon to the village. It helped assuage people’s fear and buttress their belief that the bridge could be rebuilt. In the end, the town chose honesty over avarice, and the repaired bridge became an auspicious sign of a better future.

Main Idea 主旨句题

Q. Which sentence best states the main idea of the passage?

点击显示答案

Even after a calamity, honesty, courage, and art can help a community overcome fear and rebuild hope.
